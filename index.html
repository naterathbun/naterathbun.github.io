About Nathan


Who am I? My name is Nathan Rathbun, I'm a .Net bootcamp student at Tech Elevator in Cleveland, Ohio. I graduated from Michigan State University and have almost a decade in international business experience. I've worked for the Nissan Motor and Lear Corporations in supply-chain management, and spent several years living and working in Japan. My passion is technical-problem solving and I have a life-long love of design. In my free time, I run adventure races and marathons, and travel to as many new countries as I possibly can. Upon graduating from Tech Elevator, I want to find a company where I can really come into my own as a developer and work under a strong mentor alongside a driven team. I draw excitement from the new and the unknown, so it's absolutely vital to me that I'm able to continue learning and growing every day. If I can find a company that makes that possible, I know I can contribute to their success.

My Technical Skill Set: C# .Net Framework, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and JQuery, ASP.Net MVC, Git and version control, JSON, SQL, Visual Studio, LUA and Scripting, Object-Oriented Programming and Design, Pair Programming, Test-Driven Development and TSLC practices, basic web application security.
