About


Who am I? My name is Nathan Rathbun, I'm a developer in Cleveland, Ohio. I graduated from Michigan State University and have almost a decade in international business experience. I've worked for the Nissan Motor and Lear Corporations in supply-chain management, and spent several years living and working in Japan. My passion is technical-problem solving and I have a life-long love of design. In my free time, I run adventure races and marathons, and travel to as many new countries as I possibly can. I'm a huge movie buff, and am always tinkering with electronics. I draw excitement from the new and the unknown, so it's absolutely vital to me that I'm able to continue learning and growing every day.
